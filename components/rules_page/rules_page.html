Game rules

Hello little friend, welcome to this simple but fun game. Before you start to play, please, read the game's rules carefully!

  1. The firts you will see a field with cards that upside-down
  2. Your should find a card with the same pictures for 1 minutes
  3. You can turn two cards one by one by clicking on them
  4. If you find the same card you get 1 score, if you find all cards for 1 minutes - you win
  5. If you don't, please, don't be upset and try again.
  6. Are you ready?
